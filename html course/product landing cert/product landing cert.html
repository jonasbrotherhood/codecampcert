About Strickland Bees

Strickland bees is a subscription service designed to let anyone start making honey at home. With a monthly delivery of bees and supplies, having homemade honey that is afforadable is no longer a problem.


We responsibly source all of out bees from the attics of abandoned homes. To ensure out customer sastifaction and to reduce our enviromental impact. All bees are put through our 2 week "don't sting anyone" course to guarantee our customer's safety.
