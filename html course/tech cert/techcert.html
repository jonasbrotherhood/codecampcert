Introduction

Arepas are a delicious traditional food found in Central America. It is considered to be one of the most influental dishes in the world.

Arepas are flat round cakes made from masa, and are typically filled with various ingredients.

History

The origin of the arepa is highly debated still, but the most common story is of an ancient village in present-day Venezula.

In the village were three strong warriors who were tasked to make the princess a snack. The warriors knew if the failed they would be executed. The first one brought arepas and everyone forgot about the other two warriors because the arepas were so good.

The situations

Many consider the arepa to be a sacred dish that can only be eaten at specific times and situations, but in modern time it is no longer taboo to eat arepas outside of the traditional arepa rooms.

Today people have found the arepa to be a convient and portable snack that can be eaten in any situation.
